# Mostafa-Qoli Khan E'temad al-Saltaneh — S0001 Reconciled

**Status:** Silver — complete for Source S0001  
**Branch:** Hajilu / Amir Nezam of Latgah

## Identity and family
Son of Mohammad-Baqer Khan and father of Abdollah Khan Sa'ed al-Saltaneh.

## Career
Commander of the Fadavi military establishment; active in the Anglo-Persian southern theater; negotiated at Bushehr; commanded in Astarabad; granted E'temad al-Saltaneh after that service; appointed head of the Khorasan military establishment; later governor of Urmia.

## Identity exclusions
Not Mohammad-Hasan Khan E'temad al-Saltaneh, the court historian; not Mostafa-Qoli Khan Qashqai.

## Unresolved
Exact birth/death dates, full estate holdings, exact Urmia tenure, and original title decree.
