# Mirza Abu al-Qasem Khan Naser al-Molk Gharagozloo — S0001 Reconciled

**Status:** Silver — complete for Source S0001  
**Branch:** Ashiqloo / Naser al-Molk of Bahar

## Identity and lineage
Son of Ahmad Khan Sartip and grandson/successor of Mahmoud Khan Naser al-Molk.

## Career
Educated at Oxford; served as court translator, diplomatic envoy, head of the royal arsenal, finance minister, governor of Kurdistan, prime minister, and regent during Ahmad Shah’s minority.

## Political interpretation
The author portrays him as an educated but conservative landed aristocrat who regarded constitutional change as premature. This is classified as authorial interpretation.

## Internal conflict
Chapter 2 calls Mehdi Khan his son in a regimental passage, while Chapter 4 states that his children were Hossein-Ali, Mohsen, and one daughter. The conflict remains unresolved.

## Identity exclusion
Not Mahmoud Khan Naser al-Molk, his grandfather and predecessor.
