# Mohtaj-Ali Khan Ejlal al-Mamalek / Amir Arfa' — S0001 Reconciled

**Status:** Silver — complete for Source S0001  
**Branch:** Hajilu / Amir Nezam of Latgah

## Secure findings
Son of Abdollah Khan. Brother of Mansur-Ali and, by combined textual evidence, Hossein-Qoli. Confirmed titles: Ejlal al-Mamalek and Amir Arfa'.

## Evidence limitation
S0001 supplies little independent political or military biography. The book securely establishes lineage, family-house context, and titles but not a detailed career.

## Family testimony
Gholam-Ali and Gholamhossein are recorded as his sons based on family testimony; the small genealogy diagram is supporting evidence only.

## Unresolved
Dates, spouse, office, personal estates, exact title-grant context for Amir Arfa', and documentary confirmation of descendants.
