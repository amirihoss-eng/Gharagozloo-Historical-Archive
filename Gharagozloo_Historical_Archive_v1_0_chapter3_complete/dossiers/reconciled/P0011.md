# Mohammad-Hossein Khan Gharagozloo — S0001 Reconciled

**Status:** Silver — complete for Source S0001  
**Branch:** Ashiqloo

## Identity
Early Ashiqloo chief, Zand ally, governor of Hamadan, and early-Qajar commander. He must not be merged with the later Mohammad-Hossein Khan of the Nosrat al-Molk/Hesam al-Molk line.

## Family
The narrative explicitly names five sons: Rostam Khan, Abdollah Khan Sarem al-Saltaneh, Nabi Khan, Fazlollah Khan, and Ali-Naqi Khan. Shahbaz Khan is only a probable father or predecessor.

## Career
The book associates him with Zand military service, the government of Zahab, command toward Kohgiluyeh, service under Aqa Mohammad Khan, the defense of Kerman, governorship of Hamadan, and later ministry under Dowlatshah.

## Unresolved
Exact dates; Shahbaz relationship; whether any direct genealogical link to Haji Mohammad Khan Jeyhunabadi can be proved.
