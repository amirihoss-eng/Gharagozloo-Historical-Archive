# Abdollah Khan Sardar Akram / Haji Amir Nezam — S0001 Reconciled

**Status:** Silver — complete for Source S0001  
**Branch:** Hajilu / Amir Nezam of Latgah

## Family
Son of Mostafa-Qoli Khan E'temad al-Saltaneh. Father of Hossein-Qoli Khan, Mohtaj-Ali Khan, and Mansur-Ali Khan.

## Offices and property
Commander in the Fadavi succession; governor of Khuzestan; associated with Latgah and Kabudarahang. The author reports approximately 120 landed properties late in his life.

## Political context
The author presents him as a powerful landed conservative and opponent of constitutional reform. Grain-withholding allegations are preserved as contemporary accusations, not neutral proof.

## Identity exclusion
Not Abdollah Khan Sarem al-Saltaneh of the Ashiqloo line.
