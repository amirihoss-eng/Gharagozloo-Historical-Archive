# Archive Schema v2.0

## Purpose

Version 2.0 expands the archive beyond people/events/claims so Chapter 2's institutional, military, economic, and geographic evidence can be represented without flattening it into prose.

## New entities

- `organizations` and `organization_names`: tribe, branches, families, military units, government bodies.
- `organization_memberships`: persons linked to branches and institutions.
- `military_units`: regiment-specific details.
- `roles`: normalized ranks, offices, occupations, and estate roles.
- `person_role_assignments`: a person holding a role in a place or organization over time.
- `military_commands`: explicit command succession and dates.
- `estates` and `estate_associations`: landed centers and their links to persons or family organizations.
- `entity_citations`: citations for any v2 structured entity.

## Evidence policy

Narrative text and explicit captions are primary evidence. Genealogy diagrams, especially small or degraded images, are corroborative only unless independently confirmed.
