# Master Reconciliation Wave 1

Wave 1 reconciles seven central people:

1. Mohammad-Hossein Khan Gharagozloo, early Ashiqloo chief
2. Mostafa-Qoli Khan E'temad al-Saltaneh
3. Abdollah Khan Sardar Akram / Haji Amir Nezam
4. Mohtaj-Ali Khan Ejlal al-Mamalek / Amir Arfa'
5. Gholam-Ali Khan Gharagozloo
6. Gholamhossein Khan Amiri Gharagozloo
7. Mirza Abu al-Qasem Khan Naser al-Molk

## Quality level

All seven are marked **Silver — complete for Source S0001**.

“Complete for source” does not mean that every biographical fact is known. It means:
- every currently identifiable S0001 mention was reconciled;
- identity collisions were reviewed;
- relationship evidence was classified;
- major timeline and citation issues were reviewed;
- remaining gaps were registered as research questions.

## Important corrections

- Naser al-Molk is son of Ahmad Khan Sartip and grandson of Mahmoud Khan Naser al-Molk.
- The book does not provide a reliable independent biography for Gholam-Ali or Gholamhossein Khan Amiri.
- Bibliographic references to Gholamhossein Gharagozloo must not be assigned to the archive owner’s grandfather.
- Mehdi Khan’s alleged parentage by Naser al-Molk remains internally inconsistent within S0001.
