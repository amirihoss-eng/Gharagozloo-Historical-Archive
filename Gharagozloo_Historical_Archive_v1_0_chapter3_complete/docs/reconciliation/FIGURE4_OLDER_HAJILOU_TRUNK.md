# Figure 4 — Older Hajilou Trunk Reconstruction

## Human-verified label readings

- Haji Mohammad Jafar Khan Gharagozloo
- Haji Abdollah Khan Gharagozloo
- Haji Fazlollah Khan Gharagozloo
- Nasrollah Khan Gharagozloo
- Haji Safar Khan Gharagozloo
- Haji Mina Khan Kabudarahangi
- Haji Mohammad Khan Jeyhunabadi

## Prose corroboration

S0001, printed pp. 58–59:

- identifies Haji Mohammad Jafar Khan as Hajilou chief in the Zand period;
- explicitly calls Haji Abdollah Khan his son;
- states Haji Abdollah had five male children;
- explicitly traces Haji Safar Khan to Haji Abdollah;
- names the remaining early Hajilou figures in the same passage.

## Internal source conflict

The prose appears to name Haji Mina Khan among the sons of Haji Mohammad Jafar
Khan, while Figure 4 places him among the five sons of Haji Abdollah Khan.
The archive preserves this as CF004 and Q0025.
