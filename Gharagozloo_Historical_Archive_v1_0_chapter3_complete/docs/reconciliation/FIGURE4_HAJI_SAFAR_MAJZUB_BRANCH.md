## Reading notes

The archive owner confirmed the five-child structure and read B, D, and E.
Two hard-to-read labels were independently corroborated by published
biographical lists of Majzub Ali Shah’s sons:

- A: Abd al-Javad
- C: Abd al-Majid

The exact descriptor following Mirza Ali-Naqi remains unresolved.
