# Hajilou–Ashiqloo Backbone Audit v1

## Decision

The canonical graph must not connect **Mohammad-Hossein Khan Gharagozloo (P0011)** as parent of **Haji Mohammad Khan Jeyhunabadi (P0001)**.

S0001 treats P0011 as an early **Ashiqloo** chief. It identifies the Jeyhunabadi–E'temad al-Saltaneh–Amir Nezam family as **Hajilou** and presents Hajilou and Ashiqloo in separate genealogical figures.

6. Later Amiri branch descendants

## Separate Ashiqloo reconstruction

P0011 remains the early Ashiqloo chief with his source-supported sons. His record is preserved; only the unsupported cross-branch parent edge is removed.

## Deliberately not done in migration 0018

No speculative older Hajilou ancestors are inserted. The connection between the Zand-era Hajilou leadership and P0001 must be reconstructed from Figure 4 and prose with confidence labels before it becomes canonical.

The archive owner's handwritten tree remains withheld for independent verification and has not influenced this correction.
